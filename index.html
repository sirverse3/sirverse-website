Arsalan Khan Zoyaistani - Student Developer and Founder

SirVerse - Student Social and Educational Platform

Arsalan Khan Zoyaistani is a 12th-grade ICS student studying at Government Graduate College Layyah, currently residing in Layyah Chak no. 133 TDA Layyah. He completed his matriculation from Government High School Mian Wala Jadeed Chak no. 133 TDA Layyah. As the founder and CEO of SirVerse, Arsalan is creating innovative educational technology solutions for students worldwide.

SirVerse App Features

SirVerse is a comprehensive student platform that provides social networking, academic note-taking, real-time chat functionality, AI-powered tutoring, and productivity tools specifically designed for students. The app helps students connect with peers, organize their studies, get academic assistance, and improve their learning outcomes.

Educational Content on YouTube

On his YouTube channel @Zoyaistani, Arsalan creates educational content covering Artificial Intelligence, social media awareness, productivity improvement, success strategies, time management, and academic excellence tips for students.

Technical Expertise

Arsalan Khan Zoyaistani specializes in full-stack development with expertise in React Native for mobile applications, Python Flask for backend development, PostgreSQL database management, UI/UX design, and educational technology solutions.
