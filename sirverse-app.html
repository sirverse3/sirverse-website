Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SirVerse

Is SirVerse really free for students?

Yes, SirVerse is completely free for all verified students. We believe educational tools should be accessible to everyone, and we're committed to keeping it free for students worldwide.

How do you verify students?

We use multiple secure verification methods including educational email verification, student ID uploads with privacy protection, and institutional partnerships to ensure a genuine student-only environment.

Can teachers use SirVerse?

Yes, verified educators can create classroom groups, share educational resources, interact with students in an academic context, and use the platform to enhance their teaching methods.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all private chats, never sell student data, and comply with global privacy regulations. Your academic information stays private and secure.

What makes SirVerse different?

SirVerse is designed specifically for students by a student. Instead of using multiple disconnected apps, everything is integrated into one platform focused on academic success and meaningful connections.

Can I use SirVerse for group projects?

Yes! SirVerse has dedicated features for group projects including shared notes, task assignment, file sharing, real-time collaboration tools, and group chat with academic context.
